Mileage Family Day Run 2016    

Great relationships are built on valuable shared memories. This year, let Mileage Family Run, create your beautiful memories with your loved ones.

There will be two parts to this Mileage Family Run; with the first run being held on Mother’s Day (8 May 2016) for the women and the finale run on Father’s Day for the men (19 June 2016).

For the very sporting parents/couples who wish to participate in the race together, we have something special lined up for you. Please refer to the table below for the categories.
