Mileage Father's Day Run 2015    

Have a Happy Father's day! Great relationships are built up in little ways. Be it a one-off accomplishment or a special tradition you would like to keep, join Mileage for our inaugural Father's Day Run! Start that bond by sharing irreplaceable memories with your child today.

Be a Super-Father! Exercise that superhero adrenaline pumping with our new "Father with a Stroller race" for great fun and an amazing experience! Imprint your child's first few wonderful memories while stocks last!

In view of the recent Nepal earthquake, Mileage will be trying to make a little difference by contributing all profits of the event to bringing aid to affected victims.
