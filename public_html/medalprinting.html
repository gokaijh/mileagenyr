Medal Printing



We manufacture high quality medals and coins, which are fully customistable. The design of the medal or coin is completely flexible and up to your imagination. All you have to do is provide us with your designs or ideas and we will deliver.

However, we require a mould for each customised design. Therefore, you will need to order a large number to make it cost effective.

Minimum Order: 100pcs

Turnaround time: 4-6weeks (varies depending on the complexity of the design and size of the order)

Please feel free to Contact Us if you have any queries OR Request a quote.
