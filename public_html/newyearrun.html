Mileage New Year Run 2015    

As part of Mileage’s community outreach effort, we are organizing a run on 2015 New Year. Come join us for this run and start your new year on the right foot. 50% of the proceeds will be donated.

Pacers for sub 40min, 45min, 50min, 55min and 60min will be available to help you achieve that elusive PB.

Date: 1 Jan 2015, Thursday
Venue: Punggol Park – Waterfront Promenade (Click Here for the location )
Distance: 10km (Click Here for the route ) 
Cut Off Time: 1hr 40min
Registration Fee: S$15
Closing Date: Registration closed. We have reached the maximum number of participants. Thanks for supporting us.

       a. 40min
