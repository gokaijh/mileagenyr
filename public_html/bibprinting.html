Bib Printing


We provide high-quality race bib printing services.

Our bibs are printed on durable, tearproof and waterproof polypropylene (PP). We also allow customisations of race bibs.

Options for Customisation of Race Bib:

1. Dimensions
2. Single / double-sided printing
3. Colour
4. Personalised printing (ie. appropriate name of participant, race name, logo and category can be reflected)


Minimum Order: 100 pc.

Turnaround Time: 2 to 4 weeks upon confirmation of design (varies depending on the complexity of the design and the size of the order)
