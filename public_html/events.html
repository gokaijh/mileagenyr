Mileage New Year Run 2018    

New Year’s Day.

A fresh start.

A new chapter in life waiting to be written.

This New Year, take a leap of faith and start off by believing. Believe in yourself.

The theme for this year – Breaking Through the Wall – challenges all participants to stretch their limits and break through “the wall”, a crucial moment in a timed race where one’s glycogen reserve is depleted.

As such, pacers for sub 40 min, 45 min, 50 min, 55 min and 60 min will be available to 10 km runners to help participants “break through the wall” and achieve your Personal Best.

For non-competitive participants, our Family Fun Run category is the perfect opportunity for families to strengthen ties through sports. All participants in this category will be given an equal chance to win as prizes for this category will be awarded through a lucky draw.

So join us on Monday, 1st January 2018 as The Mileage New Year Run returns for its 5th edition!
