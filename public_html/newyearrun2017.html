Mileage New Year Run 2017    

Kick start your new year on the right foot- healthy and with family!

Here at Mileage we believe there's no better way to welcome the new year than by celebrating health with the people who are the most important to you!

Our Family Fun Run will be back by popular demand to encourage family participation of all ages. Remember the catch: Prizes for this category will not be awarded to the fastest family, but to families via lucky draw.  This means all participants will be given an equal chance to win and perhaps enjoy a family dinner together!

Pacers for sub 40min, 45min, 50min, 55min and 60min will be available to 10km runners to help you achieve that elusive Personal Best.

Do come down and join Mileage for our annual New Year Run on the 1st of January 2017. See you there!
