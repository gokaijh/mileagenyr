Mileage Family Day Run 2017    

The Mileage Family Run is back once again this year! Come join us at Pasir Ris Park for an evening of fun with family. For families game for a challenge, or looking to get more of that family spirit, we have more than just individual race categories – check out the family challenge and the stroller challenge.

Great relationships are built on valuable shared memories. Create beautiful memories with your loved ones this year at the Mileage Family Run 2017! We look forward to seeing you and your family there!
