About Us


 

Mileage believes that “Quality”, not necessarily quantity, is the solution to successful distance running. A well planned training program will not only help you to achieve the objective of efficient training, but it will also bring out the best of your busy lifestyle.

  

Mileage aims to help the running enthusiasts in Singapore to train effectively, race safely and stay injury free. In addition, the company aspires to raise the general as well as scientific knowledge of the sport among the general population through seminars and workshops.
