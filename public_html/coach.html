The Coach




  

Lim Kien Mau
 

Kien Mau graduated from NTU with Honours in Mechanical and Production Engineering but he chose to follow his passion and become a certified track & field coach.

His qualifcation includes International Association of Athletics Federations (IAAF) Coaches Course - Level 2 (New System) IAAF CECS Lecturers Course – Level 1 and Singapore Sports Council (SSC) - National Coaching Accreditation Programme (NCAP) - Level 2.

Kien Mau has been involved in competitive long distance running and coaching since 1996 in Malaysia and Singapore. He believes in the importance of a well-planned training program and the ability to multi-task to ensure that his training is conducted with maximum efficiency.

Kien Mau looks forward to serving at Mileage and to continue spreading his passion for sports.
